I've been doing Taekwondo for nearly ten years and I love it! It's a sport that demands a lot of patience, perseverance, and strength. I even have a 1st degree black belt from the World Tae Kwon Do Federation!

Classical Guitar

Another hobby that I've been doing for most of my life is classical guitar. I remember when I was still a little boy and my mom asked me if I wanted to learn guitar, and I was so happy... until I realized that classical guitar meant I didn't get to strum and play chords and stuff like all of the cool kids. After playing it for 10 years, though, I realize that classical guitar's way cooler. Right now I'm working on Chopin's Nocturne Opus 9 No. 2!
