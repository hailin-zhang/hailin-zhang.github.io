A New Job

It is a period of civil war. Recruiter spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the job-seeking Hai Lin.

During the battle, Recruiter spies managed to steal secret plans to Hai Lin's ultimate weapon, the RESUME, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet.

Pursued by Hai Lin's sinister agents, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her company and restore freedom to the industry...
