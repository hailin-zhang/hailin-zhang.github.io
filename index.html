A New Job

It is a period of civil war. Recruiter spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the job-seeking Hai Lin.

During the battle, Recruiter spies managed to steal secret plans to Hai Lin's ultimate weapon, the RESUME, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet.

Pursued by Hai Lin's sinister agents, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her company and restore freedom to the industry...

Projects Jean Around the World Jean Around the World is a web app that allows users to plan an adventure anywhere in the world. Built using a MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) stack in a team of 4 students at the University of British Columbia. The app takes in a user-specified location and radius to query the Google Places API, creating a path of restaurants, parks, etc., based on the user's preference! ICBSeat ICBSeat is an office-sharing web application sponsored by ICBC that was built in a team of 7 UBC students. Using a React, Java (Spring Boot), and PostgreSQL stack, the application handles administrator functionalities such as room management and allows users to book and sign their own workspace up for bookings.
